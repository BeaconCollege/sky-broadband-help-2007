Forgotten Password

If you have forgotten, misplaced, or cannot recall your McAfee Privacy Service administrator password, please follow the instructions below. First you will be asked to attempt to answer a security question to retrieve your password. If you fail to answer this question correctly, the only option available is to uninstall McAfee Privacy Service from Safe Mode then reinstall. This action will provide the option to reset the administrator password.

  1. Attempt to answer the Privacy Service Security Question

    1. Right-click the McAfee Security Center icon () located by your system clock.
    2. Highlight Privacy Service.
    3. Click Sign In.
    4. If you do not know or cannot remember your administrator password, click "Forgot your password?".
    5. When prompted, answer the question and click Get Password.
    6. If you answered correctly, please use this password to log into Privacy Service.

    Note: If you cannot retrieve your Privacy Service Administrator password, please proceed with the next steps.

     

  2. Uninstall McAfee Privacy Service from Safe Mode

    1. Click on the Start button. The Start menu will appear.
    2. Select Shut Down from the menu. The 'Shut Down Windows' dialog box will appear.
    3. Select Shut down and click the Yes (or OK) button.
    4. Wait until the 'It is now safe to turn off the computer' message appears and turn the computer off.
    5. Turn the computer back on.
    6. Begin immediately pressing the F8 key, every other second, until the Windows Startup menu appears.
    7. Press 3 and then Enter to start the computer in Safe Mode.
    8. Once Windows starts, an information message will appear explaining Safe Mode. Click the OK button to clear this message.
    9. Proceed to Add or Remove Programs located in the Control Panel.
    10. Once completed, reboot the PC.
    11. You can now proceed to reinstall McAfee Privacy Service and specify the administrator password.
    12. Please make note of the password you specify.
