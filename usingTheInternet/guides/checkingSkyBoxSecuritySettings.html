Step 3

To check the security settings on your Sky wireless router, follow the steps below:

  1. Open a web browser window (e.g. Internet Explorer), enter 192.168.0.1 in the address bar and hit ‘Return

     

  2. A login box will pop up – enter:

    • Username: admin
    • Password: sky

  3. You will then be presented with the screen below:

  4. In the menu on the left hand side of the screen, click on Block Sites (in the Security section), and check that the settings are as shown:

  5. In the menu on the left hand side of the screen, click on Firewall Rules (in the Security section), and check that the settings are as shown:

  6. If you have anything else listed, reset the Sky wireless router to its default settings:

    1. Ensure your Sky wireless router is on
    2. Locate the small reset button on the back next to where the power cord plugs into the wireless router
    3. Using a blunt pointed object (such as a pen), press and hold in the reset button until the test light blinks, then release the button
    4. The Sky wireless router will now restart, and will be reset to the Sky Broadband default settings.
