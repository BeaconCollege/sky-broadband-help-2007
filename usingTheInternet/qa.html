Enjoying Sky Online

There are loads of great services and features available for Sky customers online. You can find out all about them on Sky.com, but in the meantime here's a taste of what you can do.


OpenDiscover Sky.com

Sky.com is your gateway to great Sky services, features and content. Make it your home page and enjoy fast access to Sky email and all your other tools. Register for Sky Player and Remote Record, or manage your bill with My Sky, and get answers to common questions.

You can also find what you're looking for with Sky Search enhanced by Google, with instant access to Google web results.

Our best content ever
Sky.com also brings you richer and more rewarding content. Keep up to date with breaking news, sport and showbiz headlines. Book your next holiday with Sky Travel or even find your next car with Sky Motoring.
Go to Sky.com

OpenSky Player

Sky Player is the home of Sky TV online – it's Sky, but on your computer. Sky Player offers programmes and movies from 25 top channels to view live on demand. Get more value from your Sky TV subscription with extra ways to watch great entertainment online. Many programmes are free, depending on your Sky TV subscription, and there's even more for you to rent or buy.

It's really easy to get started and only takes a few moments – just go to your My Sky account and log in using your username and password and then download the Sky Player application.
