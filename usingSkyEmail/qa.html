Using Sky Email and Tools

Sky Email and Tools has everything you need to keep in touch, stay connected and get organised, with great features like Chat for instant messaging and a handy Calendar, plus Photos courtesy of Snapfish so you can save and share memories.

 OpenHow do I set up Sky Email and Tools?

Included with your Sky Broadband you get all of the following great services:

Sky Email
Sky Email gives you:

  • Virtually unlimited storage (10GB*), so you'll never have to delete another email
  • Advanced Google search technology - finding emails has never been simpler
  • Unique 'conversations' views - so you always have a record of who said what, when
  • Up to 10 sky.com email addresses for you and your loved ones.
  • The ability to access your Sky Email from any computer connected to the internet if you are away or on holiday.

Just go Tools to sign in.

It's easy to set up email addresses for your friends and family - you've got up to 10 sky.com accounts with virtually unlimited storage so everyone in your home can have their own account. You won't have to wade through a shared mailbox to find messages meant for you. To add new users to your account simply log in to My Sky

Moving existing email accounts to Sky Email is simple with our Email Switcher tool - it will copy all your contacts over to your new Sky Email and then with a few simple clicks you can let everyone know about your new sky.com email address. To switch your email now just log in to Tools

Sky Calendar
Set up your calendar online and share it with friends and family, who can share their calendars too. And never miss important meetings or birthdays, with reminders by email or straight to your mobile.

Sky Chat
Sky Chat lets you catch up with friends online, so you can:

  • Instantly message your contacts when they're online
  • See who's online for a chat with Quick Contacts
  • Save, search, forward and store your chats just like emails

You can also use Sky Chat online even when you're not logged into your email, with our new Sky Chat gadget - find out more on Tools

Sky Photos
Sky Broadband working with Snapfish allows you to keep, share and play with all your photos at the touch of a button.

You can upload photos straight from your digital camera and camera phone to your Sky Photos account, so you can upload your photos before your memory card fills up. You can share your pictures online as well, so you don't need to fill your friends' email inboxes with huge photo attachments - just send them to Sky Photos to see your pics!

As your photographic handiwork will be on display, we help you make the most of your efforts with customised photo albums, themed borders and nifty editing tools. You can convert your colour photos to cool, artistic black and white, crop images and create outstanding images that do justice to your treasured memories, crazy lifestyle shots and any scenes that capture your interest.
