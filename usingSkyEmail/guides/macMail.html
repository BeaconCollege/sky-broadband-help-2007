Mail

Guide to setting up your Sky email in Mail for Macs (OSX)

  1. Open Mail Setup Wizard and click Continue

  2. Select POP from the “Account Type” drop down menu. You may enter a description of the account in the “Account Description” box, i.e. “my sky email” or anything of your choosing. Type your name in the Full Name box (will appear as the ‘from’ name on emails you send). Enter your email address (e.g.: john.smith@sky.com) in lowercase letters, with no spaces, in the Email Address box. Click Continue

  3. In the ‘Incoming mail server’ box enter: pop.sky.com (all lowercase, no spaces). In the ‘User Name’ box enter the first part of your @sky.com email address (everything before the @ sign). In the Password box, enter the password for your @sky.com email account. Click Continue

  4. In the ‘Outgoing mail server’ box enter: smtp.sky.com (all lowercase, no spaces). Leave the “Use Authentication” tick box Unchecked. Username and password boxes should be left empty. Click Continue

  5. Check the account summary looks all ok. Click Continue

  6. Click Done

     

  7. If you would like to set up additional accounts, click the “Create Another Account” button and repeat the above steps

Troubleshooting

If the wizard does not start when opening Mail, you need to do the following:

  1. Open Mail, click the Mail menu, then select Preferences

  2. Click on the “Accounts” tab at the top. Click on the “+” towards the bottom left.

  3. Select POP from the “Account Type” drop down menu. You may enter a description of the account in the “Account Description” box, i.e. “my sky email” or anything of your choosing.
