Outlook 2007

Guide to setting up your Sky email in Outlook 2007

  1. Open Outlook, click [Tools] then select Accounts Settings

  2. To add a new email account, make sure you have selected the E-mail tab, and click New

  3. Tick Manually Configure server settings or additional server types, click Next

  4. Select Internet E-mail, click Next

  5. Type your name in the Your Name box (will appear as the ‘from’ name on emails you send).

    Enter your email address (e.g.: john.smith@sky.com) in lowercase letters, with no spaces, in the E-mail Address box.

    Leave the Account Type configured as POP3.

    In the ‘Incoming mail server’ box enter: pop.sky.com (all lowercase, no spaces).

    In the ‘Outgoing mail server’ box enter: smtp.sky.com (all lowercase, no spaces).

    In the ‘User name’ box enter the first part of your @sky.com email address (everything before the @ sign).

    In the Password box, enter the password for your @sky.com email account. Tick the “Remember password” box if you would like Outlook to remember your password so that you do not need to enter it each time you check for any new email.

    Leave the Require logon using Secure Password Authentication (SPA) box unchecked.

     

  6. Click Next

  7. Click Finish

  8. You will now see your account has been added to the list of configured E-mail accounts.

  9. Repeat the above process for each of your sky.com email accounts
