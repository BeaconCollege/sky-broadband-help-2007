Outlook Express

Guide to setting up your Sky email in Outlook Express (versions 5 and 6)

  1. Open Outlook Express, click Tools, then select Accounts

  2. Click the Mail tab, then click Add and select Mail

  3. The Internet Connection Wizard will start. Type in your Display Name (will appear as the ‘from’ name on emails you send – e.g. ‘John Smith’) then click Next

  4. Enter your email address (e.g.: john.smith@sky.com) in lowercase letters, with no spaces, and then click Next

  5. Leave ‘My incoming mail server is a [POP3] server’ drop down box as POP3. In ‘Incoming mail server’ enter: pop.sky.com (all lowercase, no spaces). In ‘Outgoing mail (SMTP) server’ enter: smtp.sky.com (all lowercase, no spaces).

    Click Next

  6. In the ‘Account name’ box enter the first part of your @sky.com email address (everything before the @ sign). In the Password box, enter the password for your @sky.com email account. Tick the “Remember password” box if you would like Outlook Express to remember your password so that you do not need to enter it each time you check for any new email. Leave the “Log on using Secure Password Authentication (SPA)” box unchecked.

    Click Next

  7. Click Finish

  8. Repeat the above process for each of your sky.com email accounts.
