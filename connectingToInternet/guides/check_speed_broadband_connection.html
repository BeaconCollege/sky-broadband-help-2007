Check the speed of your broadband connection

To check the speed of your broadband connection, follow the steps below.

  1. Open a web browser window (e.g. Internet Explorer), enter 192.168.0.1 in the address bar, and press Return.

  2. In the log-in box, enter username: admin and password: sky.

  3. You will then see the screen below. The highlighted area shows where you can see your broadband connection speeds.
